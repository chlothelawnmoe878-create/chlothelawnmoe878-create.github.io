The Only Skincare Routine You Actually Need in 2026

Forget the 12-step routine. Here's what dermatologists actually recommend — simplified, affordable, and effective.

Let's be honest — skincare TikTok has made everything way more complicated than it needs to be. Double cleansing, seven serums, slugging, skin cycling… it's a lot.

The truth? Most dermatologists agree on a shockingly simple routine. Here's the no-BS version that actually works.

Morning Routine (3 Steps)

1. Gentle Cleanser — You don't need to strip your skin every morning. A simple rinse with water or a gentle cleanser is all you need.

What You Can Skip

  • Toner — Unless it's a treatment toner, it's mostly just expensive water
  • Eye cream — Your regular moisturizer works fine around the eyes
  • 10 different serums — Pick 1-2 actives max. Your skin can only absorb so much
  • Expensive brands — CeraVe and The Ordinary exist for a reason

The Bottom Line

Great skin isn't about having 15 products. It's about being consistent with the right 3-4. Cleanse, treat, moisturize, protect. That's it. Save your money for the stuff that actually matters.
